About Me

Data Engineer with a strong foundation in Big Data having four years of experience as a Senior Engineer at Mindtree and as a Specialist Software Engineer at Société Générale. Proficient in managing and processing large-scale data using big data technologies such as Spark, Hadoop, Kafka, and Oozie.

In addition to my technical expertise, I am adept in multiple programming languages such as Scala, Python, SQL, and R. My skills extend to deploying applications using Jenkins on big data clusters, utilizing cloud storage services like S3 and RDS, and working with cloud platforms for clients in the areas of Banking and Hospitality Industry. My professional credentials include certifications in Machine Learning(IBM), Data Science (IBM), Data Analytics (Google), and Statistics.

In addition to my work experience, I have also worked on projects such as predicting successful landings for Falcon 9 first-stage boosters to building a data vault for storing and analysing medical imaging data. My achievements include the "A-Team" Award six times from Mindtree Ltd for collaborative team spirit and "Academic Excellence" in my bachelor's degree.

Currently, I am pursuing a Master of Science degree in Data Science at the University of Birmingham, where my academic interests align with my enthusiasm for utilizing data-driven insights to tackle interesting problems. I am always seeking new opportunities to apply my skills and experience to drive impactful outcomes.
