About Me

Data Engineer with a strong foundation in Big Data having four and half years of experience in IT Services. Proficient in managing and processing large-scale data using big data technologies such as Spark, Hadoop, Kafka, and Oozie.

In addition to my technical proficiency, I am skilled in various programming languages including Scala, Python, SQL, and R. My skills extend to deploying applications using Jenkins on big data clusters, utilizing cloud storage services like S3 and RDS, and working with cloud platforms for clients in the areas of Finance, Banking and Hospitality Industry. My professional credentials include certifications in Machine Learning(IBM), Data Science (IBM), Data Analytics (Google), and Statistics.

I am always seeking new opportunities to apply my skills and experience to drive impactful outcomes.
